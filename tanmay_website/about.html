Tanmay Agnihotri

Jai gaur

Shri Radharamano Vijayate By the divine grace of Sri Radharaman dev ju and Guru Gaurang, Tanmay completed his visharad diploma degree in hindustani shastriya sangeet vidya from Bhatkhande Sangeet Vidyapith Lucknow.

He is a deciple of SrimanmadhvaGaudeshwar vaishnavacharya paramaradhya sadgurudev shri Pundrik Goswami Ji Maharaj under Brahma Madhva Gaudiya Parampara (sri Radharaman Tample vrindavan).

He started his singing passion in his life from he was just 8 years old in Devotionl and western feild. He got a huge support to initiate his footsteps in his passion of music from his whole family and school.


Mainly he is a devotional, classical, semi classical and bollywod singer. Also an instrumentalist in Piano, Harmonium, Guitar, Dholak, Mridangam etc.
The conversion of his passion into profession was in the year 2021 by the first Sankirtan event in Pracheen Meerabai Tample by the grace of sri Radharaman dev ju. Also he is teaching in his online classes named: Radharaman Music Academy.

Tanmay performed many stage programs of Sankirtan events like Twice in Pracheen Meerabai Tample vrindavan Dham Named Kartik Mahotsav. Another one in the city Panchalnagar (Farrukhabad) Sri Pandaveshwar mahadev Tample, by the name of Smriti Mahotsav.

Also in the city Rewari, Agra , shridham Ayodhya , and so on.By the Holy grace of sri Radharaman ji, he is continuously spreading Harinaam in various cities. other contact info are given below.

Jai Jai sri Radhe.
